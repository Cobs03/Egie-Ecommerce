## Troubleshooting

### Common Cart Issues

#### ‚ùå "Cannot Add Item to Cart"

**Possible Causes:**
1. **Not Signed In**
   - Solution: Sign in to your account first
   
2. **Out of Stock**
   - Solution: Check stock status, wait for restock
   
3. **Variant Not Selected**
   - Solution: Select size/color/variant before adding
   
4. **Network Error**
   - Solution: Check internet connection, try again

#### ‚ùå "Cart is Empty" (But You Added Items)

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you're signed into the same account
2. Check if browser cookies are enabled
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Re-add items if necessary

#### ‚ùå "Item Removed from Cart"

**Possible Reasons:**
- Product went out of stock
- Product was deleted by seller
- Price changed significantly
- Promotional period ended

**Solution:**
- Browse similar products
- Contact support for alternatives
- Check back later for restock

#### ‚ùå "Cannot Update Quantity"

**Solutions:**
1. Check if exceeding stock limit
2. Ensure quantity is at least 1
3. Refresh page and try again
4. Remove and re-add item

### Common Checkout Issues

#### ‚ùå "Please Select Delivery Method"

**Solution:**
- Go back to cart page
- Click either "Local Delivery" or "Store Pickup"
- Method must be highlighted before checkout
- Return to checkout

#### ‚ùå "Please Select at Least One Item"

**Solution:**
- Go back to cart
- Check the checkbox next to items you want to purchase
- At least one item must be selected
- Click "Proceed to Checkout" again

#### ‚ùå "Please Select Delivery Address"

**For Local Delivery orders:**

**Solutions:**
1. Click "+ Add New Address" if first time
2. Fill all required fields completely
3. Click "Save Address"
4. Click "Select" on the address
5. Proceed to payment

#### ‚ùå "Address Incomplete"

**Missing Fields:**
- Check all required fields marked with *
- Select province, city, and barangay from dropdowns
- Enter street address and postal code
- Click "Save Address"

### Common Payment Issues

#### ‚ùå "Payment Method Not Selected"

**Solution:**
- Scroll to payment section
- Click on Credit Card, GCash, or COD
- Option should highlight when selected
- Try placing order again

#### ‚ùå "Invalid Card Details"

**Credit Card Issues:**
1. **Card Number**:
   - Check for typos
   - Ensure 15-16 digits
   - No letters or special characters
   
2. **Expiry Date**:
   - Must be future date
   - Use MM/YY format (e.g., 12/25)
   
3. **CVV**:
   - 3 digits for Visa/Mastercard
   - 4 digits for American Express
   - Found on back of card

#### ‚ùå "Card Declined"

**Possible Reasons:**
1. Insufficient funds
2. Card expired
3. Incorrect details entered
4. Bank security block
5. Card not enabled for online transactions

**Solutions:**
- Contact your bank
- Try different card
- Use alternative payment method (GCash/COD)
- Verify card details are correct

#### ‚ùå "GCash Payment Failed"

**Common Issues:**
1. **Insufficient Balance**
   - Check GCash balance
   - Load more funds
   
2. **Incorrect PIN**
   - Enter correct GCash PIN
   - Reset PIN if forgotten
   
3. **Payment Timeout**
   - Complete within time limit
   - Start checkout process again
   
4. **Network Error**
   - Check internet connection
   - Try again after few minutes

#### ‚ùå "Payment Pending"

**For GCash:**
- Payment may take a few minutes to verify
- Check your email for confirmation
- Contact support if pending >30 minutes
- Check GCash transaction history

**For COD:**
- Status remains "Pending" until delivery
- This is normal for COD orders
- Payment due on delivery/pickup

### Page/Loading Issues

#### ‚ùå "Checkout Page Not Loading"

**Solutions:**
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try different browser
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Check internet connection
5. Try on different device

#### ‚ùå "Stuck on Processing"

**If Payment Screen Freezes:**
1. **DO NOT** refresh immediately
2. Wait 2-3 minutes
3. Check email for confirmation
4. Check "My Purchases" for order
5. Contact support if unsure

**If Order Duplicated:**
- Only one order will process
- Payment gateway prevents duplicates
- Check email for actual order count
- Contact support to cancel duplicate if needed

## FAQs

### General Cart Questions

**Q: How long do items stay in my cart?**
- A: Items remain in cart indefinitely while signed in
- May be removed if product becomes unavailable
- Cart syncs across devices with same account

**Q: Can I save items for later without adding to cart?**
- A: Use the wishlist feature (bookmark icon)
- Or keep items in cart without checking them out
- Cart items remain until you remove them

**Q: Can I edit my cart during checkout?**
- A: Click "Back" or "Return to Cart" button
- Make changes in cart page
- Return to checkout when ready

**Q: Do prices change after adding to cart?**
- A: Prices lock when you add to cart
- You pay the price shown in cart
- Promotions are preserved

**Q: Can I apply multiple discount codes?**
- A: Typically only one voucher per order
- System will apply best available discount
- Check terms of specific promotions

### Delivery Questions

**Q: What's the difference between delivery methods?**

| Feature | Local Delivery | Store Pickup |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| **Fee** | ‚Ç±100 | Free |
| **Time** | 2-5 business days | Immediate (after processing) |
| **Address** | Required | Not required |
| **Best For** | Convenience | Save money |

**Q: Can I change delivery method after ordering?**
- A: Contact support immediately
- May be possible if order not yet processed
- No guarantee if already in preparation

**Q: What if I'm not home during delivery?**
- A: Delivery person will call/text
- Can schedule re-delivery
- Or arrange pickup from courier office
- Update delivery instructions in order notes

**Q: Can someone else pick up my order?**
- A: Yes, with authorization
- Provide order number and ID
- May need authorization letter
- Contact store for requirements

### Payment Questions

**Q: Which payment method is fastest?**
- A: Credit card and GCash process instantly
- COD requires payment on delivery
- All methods secure and reliable

**Q: Are my payment details safe?**
- A: Yes, all payments processed through secure gateways
- SSL encryption protects data
- We don't store card details
- PCI DSS compliant

**Q: Can I cancel after payment?**
- A: Depends on order status
- Contact support immediately
- May be cancelable if not yet processed
- Refund processed to original payment method

**Q: What if payment deducted but order failed?**
- A: Contact support with transaction details
- Payment gateway will reverse if order not created
- Allow 5-7 business days for reversal
- Provide screenshot/proof of payment

**Q: Can I use international credit cards?**
- A: Check with support for international card acceptance
- Philippine-issued cards preferred
- Currency conversion may apply
- Additional bank fees possible

### Order Questions

**Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?**
- A: Contact support immediately
- May be possible before processing starts
- Cannot modify after shipment
- May need to cancel and reorder

**Q: How do I track my order?**
- A: Check "My Purchases" page
- Order status updates automatically
- Email notifications for key milestones
- Contact support for detailed tracking

**Q: What if I receive wrong/damaged items?**
- A: Document with photos immediately
- Contact support within 24-48 hours
- Keep all packaging
- Replacement or refund processed

**Q: Can I return items?**
- A: Check return policy in Terms & Conditions
- Usually 7-14 days for returns
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Contact support to initiate return

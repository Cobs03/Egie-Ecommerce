## Password Recovery

### Forgot Your Password?

If you can't remember your password, follow these steps:

#### Step 1: Access Password Reset
- On the **Sign In** page, click **"Forgot Password"** link
- Located below the password field

#### Step 2: Enter Your Email
- Type the email address associated with your account
- Click **"Send Reset Email"** or **"Reset Password"** button

#### Step 3: Check Your Email
- Open your email inbox (check spam/junk folder if needed)
- Look for an email titled "Password Reset Request" or similar
- ‚è±Ô∏è The email should arrive within 1-5 minutes

#### Step 4: Click Reset Link
- Open the password reset email
- Click the **"Reset Password"** link in the email
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important**: This link expires after a certain time
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Mobile Users**: Use a desktop/laptop computer for best results

#### Step 5: Create New Password
- You'll be directed to the **Reset Password** page
- **New Password**: Enter your new password
  - Must meet password requirements (see Sign Up section)
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter the same password
- üëÅÔ∏è Use the eye icon to show/hide passwords

#### Step 6: Save and Sign In
- Click **"Reset Password"** or **"Update Password"**
- ‚úÖ Success! You'll see a confirmation message
- You'll be automatically redirected to the sign-in page
- Sign in with your new password

### Password Reset Not Working?

If you encounter issues:
1. **Link Expired**: Request a new password reset email
2. **Email Not Received**: 
   - Check spam/junk folders
   - Verify the email address is correct
   - Wait 5-10 minutes and try again
3. **Invalid Link Error**: Request a fresh password reset link
4. **Mobile Issues**: Switch to a desktop/laptop computer

## Troubleshooting

### Common Sign Up Issues

#### ‚ùå "Email Already Registered"
- **Problem**: An account with this email already exists
- **Solution**: 
  - Click "Log In" instead of "Sign Up"
  - Or use "Forgot Password" if you can't remember your credentials
  - Try signing in with Google if you originally used that method

#### ‚ùå "Invalid Email Address"
- **Problem**: The email format is incorrect
- **Solution**: 
  - Check for typos in your email
  - Ensure format is: username@domain.com
  - Remove any spaces before or after the email

#### ‚ùå "Password Too Weak"
- **Problem**: Password doesn't meet security requirements
- **Solution**: 
  - Use at least 6 characters
  - Include uppercase letters (A-Z)
  - Include lowercase letters (a-z)
  - Include numbers (0-9)
  - Include special characters (@, #, $, etc.)
  - Example: `MyP@ssw0rd123`

#### ‚ùå "Confirmation Email Not Received"
- **Problem**: Verification email hasn't arrived
- **Solution**: 
  1. Check your spam/junk folder
  2. Wait 5-10 minutes (emails may be delayed)
  3. Check that you entered the correct email
  4. Request a new confirmation email
  5. Add noreply@supabase.io to your safe senders list

#### ‚ùå "Google Sign-Up Failed"
- **Problem**: Google authentication didn't work
- **Solution**: 
  - Make sure pop-ups are enabled in your browser
  - Try signing up with email instead
  - Check your internet connection
  - Clear browser cache and try again
  - Use a different browser

### Common Sign In Issues

#### ‚ùå "Invalid Email or Password"
- **Problem**: Credentials don't match
- **Solution**: 
  - Check for typos in email and password
  - Remember passwords are case-sensitive
  - Use "Forgot Password" to reset
  - Try signing in with Google if you registered that way

#### ‚ùå "Email Not Confirmed"
- **Problem**: You haven't verified your email
- **Solution**: 
  - Check your email inbox for the confirmation link
  - Click the verification link in the email
  - Request a new confirmation email if needed

#### ‚ùå "Too Many Login Attempts"
- **Problem**: Multiple failed login attempts detected
- **Solution**: 
  - Wait 5-10 minutes before trying again
  - Use "Forgot Password" to reset your credentials
  - Contact support if the issue persists

#### ‚ùå "User Not Found"
- **Problem**: No account exists with this email
- **Solution**: 
  - Verify you're using the correct email
  - Click "Sign Up" to create a new account
  - Try signing in with Google if you registered that way

#### ‚ùå "Google Sign-In Failed"
- **Problem**: Google authentication error
- **Solution**: 
  - Enable pop-ups for the website
  - Make sure you're using the correct Google account
  - Try signing in with email instead
  - Clear browser cache and cookies

## Security Best Practices

### Protecting Your Account

#### 1. **Create a Strong Password**
- ‚úÖ Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols
- ‚úÖ Avoid using personal information (birthdays, names)
- ‚úÖ Don't reuse passwords from other websites
- ‚úÖ Consider using a password manager
- ‚ùå Never share your password with anyone

#### 2. **Email Security**
- ‚úÖ Use a secure email provider
- ‚úÖ Enable two-factor authentication on your email
- ‚úÖ Regularly check for suspicious login attempts
- ‚ùå Don't use public computers to access your email

#### 3. **Sign Out When Done**
- ‚úÖ Always sign out on shared or public computers
- ‚úÖ Close your browser after signing out
- ‚úÖ Don't save passwords on public devices

#### 4. **Be Aware of Phishing**
- ‚úÖ Verify email sender before clicking links
- ‚úÖ Official emails come from @yourdomain.com or @supabase.io
- ‚úÖ Check the URL in your browser (look for https://)
- ‚ùå Never enter credentials on suspicious websites
- ‚ùå Don't respond to emails asking for your password

#### 5. **Regular Account Maintenance**
- ‚úÖ Update your password every 3-6 months
- ‚úÖ Review your account activity regularly
- ‚úÖ Update your email if it changes
- ‚úÖ Keep your profile information current

#### 6. **Using Google Sign-In**
- ‚úÖ Ensure your Google account has strong security
- ‚úÖ Enable 2-step verification on Google
- ‚úÖ Review Google account permissions regularly
- ‚úÖ Use Google's account recovery options

### What to Do If Your Account Is Compromised

If you suspect unauthorized access:

1. **Immediately change your password**
   - Use "Forgot Password" feature
   - Create a completely new password

2. **Check recent activity**
   - Review your order history
   - Check your cart for unauthorized items
   - Look for unfamiliar shipping addresses

3. **Update your email password**
   - If someone accessed your account, they may access your email
   - Change your email password immediately

4. **Contact support**
   - Report the security issue
   - Request account review
   - Ask for assistance securing your account

5. **Review connected accounts**
   - If using Google sign-in, review Google account security
   - Remove any suspicious connected apps
